The Best Movies According To Pujitha

My top3 movies of all time.

Dasara

This movie is about friendship and the sacrifice of the love of the hero for his friend in the first half. The second half is about marrying his love to save her from the villain. In the second half the Acting of hero is awesome as he overcomes his fear to save his lover. Fighting scenes are super and the movie has a good message for the audience.

Karthikeya 2

This movie is about Lord Krishna and the main actors in this film have an aim of saving the krishna's belongings and protecting them from evil hands. The story line of the movie is awesome. By watching this movie we can know more about lord krishna.

Driver Jamuna

It has only Female lead. She is an taxi driver and she has good driving skills. This movie has a scene that elevates the driving skills of her which is mind blowing. In this movie she takes revange on the villain as her father died because of the villain. The story line was well written and the actress played it very well,the main thing that i like about her is her Braveness.
